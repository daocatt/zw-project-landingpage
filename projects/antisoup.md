# AntiSoup

A community-driven voting platform to rank, critique, and debunk viral 'Chicken Soup' quotes and cynical satire. Real votes, real rankings, no fluff.

## Overview
Not all advice is good advice. Antisoup creates a space where reality meets rhetoric. We invite you to cast your vote on the web's most viral motivational and demotivational quotes. Check the live leaderboard to see which quotes stand the test of logic and which ones are just... soup.
